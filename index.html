Information On Dogs

Dogs are one of the most interesting animals on the planet.

Many people love dogs. Dogs are natural descend of wolves.

When you look at dogs today you typically do not see anything resembling a wolf.

Dogs are called a mans best friend for a reason.

Many people have dogs and dogs are consider one of the most loyal animals.

There are many different types of dog classifications.

Dogs are typically classified into different breed categories,

including: non working group, toy group, herding group, and sporting group.
