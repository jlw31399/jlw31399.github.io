Information On Dogs

Dogs are one of the most interesting animals on the planet. They are loved by many and are natural descend of wolves.

When you look at dogs today you typically do not see anything resembling a wolf. In fact, dogs are typically refered to as a mans best friend.

Dogs thrive on a strach based diet and are members of the canis lupis species.

Many people have dogs consider them to be one of the most loyal animals. There are many different types of dogs and breeds of dogs.

Dogs are typically classified into different breed categories,including: non working group, toy group, herding group, and sporting group.

Personally, I love dogs. I have two French Bulldogs at home. Their names are max and lucy.

German Shepherd

German Shepherds sometimes become police dogs! It is great to see these dogs doing such hard work. They are also considered working dogs.

While many police dogs seem intimidating German Shepherds and known to be very sweet dogs.

Austrial Shepherd

These dogs are really active dogs. They love to run around and play games. They are great with families and children.

These dogs have so much energy that its hard to control them.
